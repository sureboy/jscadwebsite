(static) create()

Create a new connector. A connector allows two objects to be connected at predefined positions. For example a servo motor and a servo horn can both have a connector called 'shaft'. The horn can be moved and rotated to any position, and then the servo horn is attached to the servo motor at the proper position, such that the two connectors match. Connectors are children of the solid, transform-wise, so transformations are applied to both solid and connector(s). (parent => child relationship)
Properties:
Name Type Description
point vec3 the position of the connector (relative to its parent)
axis vec3 the direction (unit vector) of the connector
normal vec3 the direction (unit vector) perpendicular to axis, that defines the "12 o'clock" orientation of the connector
